Pet Formulas

EXP multiplier:
  level 0-39   = 2
  level 40-49  = 3
  level 50-59  = 4
  level 60-69  = 5
  level 70-79  = 6
  level 80-89  = 7
  level 90+    = 8

requiredExp(level) = multiplier * (level + 1)

statValue(level, baseValue, statType) =
  baseValue + ((baseValue / denominator) * level)

denominator:
  AttackDamage = 10
  all other pet stats = 25

Skill Formulas

EXP multiplier:
  level 0-49   = 2
  level 50-59  = 3
  level 60-69  = 4
  level 70+    = 5

requiredExp(level) = multiplier * (level + 1)

rarityMultiplier:
  Common    = 1
  Rare      = 2
  Epic      = 5
  Legendary = 10
  Mystic    = 15

bonusDamage(level, rarity) =
  level * rarityMultiplier

Known Limits

Skill Base + bonus uses the recovered Skill.BonusDamage method. Simple chain multiplies that value by extracted multiple and bounce counts. Skill.AttackForText and Skill.BackgroundPattern also touch behavior objects and PvP/game-state data, so those runtime parts are not included here.
